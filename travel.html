How To Get There

From Paris (CDG) via Avignon

Take the TGV (rail) to Avignon. The train ride duration is around 3 hours and 15 minutes. From Avignon, it's a 25-minute drive or a 45-minute bus ride. Your options are:
(1) Take bus Line 57 (of the Bouches-du-Rhône bus company LePilot) from the Avignon Centre station to Saint Rémy. To get from the TGV station to the Centre station, simply transfer to another (TER) tain. The ride is 5 minutes. From the Centre station, walk over to the Avignon Gare routière PEM bus station (3-minute walk) to catch Line 57. For the bus schedule, click here.
(2) Rent a car at the Avignon train station
(3) Take a cab from the train station

From Marseille (MRS)

It's a 1-hour drive. Your options are:
(1) Rent a car at the MRS airport
(2) Take a cab from the airport

From Nice (NCE)

It's a 2-hour and 15-minute drive. Your only option really is to rent a car.
