How To Get There

Venue and Recommended Accomodation

Le Cloître Saint-Louis
20 Rue du Portail Boquier, 84000 Avignon, France
(Five minute walk from Avignon Centre train station)

From Paris

Take the TGV from Paris Airport CDG (the train station is in the airport) or Paris Gare Lyon (if you are already in Paris) to Avignon Centre. The train takes around 3 hours and 30 minutes and requires a change of trains at Avignon TGV.

The Avignon TGV station is 5km from Avignon Centre. There are rental cars at Avignon TGV. All wedding events are within walking distance from Avignon Centre so a car is not necessary for wedding events but if you want to get a car this is a good place to get one. There is parking at Le Cloître Saint-Louis.

Train tickets can be purchased at SNCF.

Train station names will appear as follows on the SNCF site:
Paris Airport CDG: "Paris Roissy Charles de Gaulle Airport (TGV station - CDG 2 - 25km from Paris - FR)"
Avignon Centre: "Avignon Centre (FR)"
Paris Gare de Lyon: use "Paris (all stations - FR)"

From Marseille

Take the TER from Marseille St Charles to Avignon Centre. The train takes around 1 hour and 10 minutes. You can purchase train tickets SNCF.

From Nice

Take the TGV from Nice (FR) to Avignon to Avignon Centre. The train takes around 3 hours to 3 hours and 44 minutes. You can purchase train tickets SNCF.
