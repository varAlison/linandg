How To Get There

Venue / Recommended Accomodation

Le Cloitre Saint Louis
20 Rue du Portail Boquier, 84000 Avignon, France

From Paris

Take the TGV from Paris Airport CDG or Paris Gare Lyon (if you are in Paris) to Avignon TGV; from Avignon TGV, you can catch a 5-minute train or bus from Avignon TGV to Avignon Centre, or you can take a cab. The train takes around 3 hours and 30 minutes. You can purchase train tickets here. If you are taking the train from Avignon TGV to Avignon Centre, select "Avignon" as your final destination.

From Marseille

Take the TER from Marseille St Char to Avignon Centre. The train takes around 1 hour and 10 minutes. You can purchase train tickets here.

From Nice

Take the TGV from Nice-Ville to Avignon TGV; from Avignon TGV, you can catch a 5-minute train or bus from Avignon TGV to Avignon Centre, or you can take a cab. The train takes around 3 hours to 3 hours and 44 minutes. You can purchase train tickets here. If you are taking the train from Avignon TGV to Avignon Centre, select "Avignon" as your final destination.
